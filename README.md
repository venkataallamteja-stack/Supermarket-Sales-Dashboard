## Business Insights
- Food and beverages generated strong sales performance.
- Member customers contributed higher revenue than normal customers.
- Cash payments were the most frequently used payment method.
- Yangon city recorded high overall sales.

## Recommendations
- Increase promotions for top-performing product categories.
- Introduce loyalty offers for normal customers.
- Encourage digital payments with cashback offers.
- Focus marketing campaigns in high-performing cities.
